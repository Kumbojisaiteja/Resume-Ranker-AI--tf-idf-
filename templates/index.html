About Us

Our system is an AI-powered resume screening platform that analyzes resumes and matches them with job descriptions to identify the best candidates efficiently.

AI-Powered Matching:

Our system uses machine learning and NLP to evaluate how well a resume matches a given job description.


Smart Resume Scoring:

Generates a match score (0–100) based on semantic similarity, skills, and experience.


Skill Extraction:

Automatically extracts and compares skills from resumes and job descriptions.


Explainable Results:

Provides clear reasons for predictions, including missing skills and improvement suggestions.


Job Recommendation:

Suggests suitable job roles based on the candidate’s skill set.


Multi-Format Support:

Supports PDF, DOCX, TXT extenstion files for uploads.


User Friendly:

Simple interface to upload resumes and get instant analysis.
